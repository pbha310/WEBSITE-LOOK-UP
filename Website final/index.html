Find people

Why Use Look Up

Look Up helps raise awareness around smartphone addiction and phubbing. Whilst out with friends or family, use our unique feature called “Nudge a Phubber” to help restore a real life conversation with someone who has been distracted by their phones. Meet new people around you with “find people” to have a real life conversation face-to-face.

  • Reliable and Secure Platform
  • Support a genuine global cause and raise awareness around smartphone addiction
  • No dating stigma attached
